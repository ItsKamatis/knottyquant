KnottyQuant?

Knotty, naughty, all wound up? They say great things come in three, so what gives? Maybe all those 3 things are true. But what we’re here to discuss is the application of knot theory in the realm of finance. Can it really work, are there practical applications?

Knot theory is used to study entanglement in protein structures. In finance, the practical connection is broader topology and representing stock prices as strands of a braid.
