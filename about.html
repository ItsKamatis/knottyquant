About

KnottyQuant is a record of quantitative research.

The site collects projects, technical notes, and writing on derivatives, risk, portfolio construction, numerical methods, and topology.

The site

Name and approach

Knotty, naughty, all wound up? The wordplay is deliberate. The technical reference is knot theory and its use in studying entangled structures such as protein knots. The connection to finance is broader topology, particularly topological data analysis; the two fields are related but not interchangeable.

The editorial approach is simple: show assumptions, identify the data boundary, separate evidence from interpretation, retain negative results when they matter, and state what a project does not establish.
